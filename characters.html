Characters

  • Dorian Gray
  • A very handsome young gentleman, Dorian begins as a pure innocent soul. Dorian is corrupted by the philosophies of Lord Henry, the “yellow book” and the consequence free life that he lives after selling his soul for eternal youth.
  • Lord Henry Wotton
  • A selfish man that lives his life according to his desires and impulses. Encourages Dorian’s sinful behavior.
  • Basil Hallward
  • An artist that becomes enthralled by Dorian’s beauty and pure nature. He continues to have faith in Dorian until his death.
