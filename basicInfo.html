Basic Info

  • Title
  • The Picture of Dorian Gray
  • Author
  • Oscar Wilde
  • Published
  • It was published in 1891 by Ward, Lock & Company.
  • Genre
  • Comedy of manners, Gothic.
  • Setting
  • Set in the 1890's in London, England.
  • Point Of View
  • The novel is written in the third person from an unspecified omniscient narrator.
  • Conflict
  • Dorian Gray is so obsessed with remaining young and beautiful that he offers his soul in exchange for youth. Instead of aging a portrait of him takes on all the age and issues attributed to his daily life. He must cope with the decay of his portrait in front of his eyes, as it shows just how far he has fallen despite his exterior of youth.
